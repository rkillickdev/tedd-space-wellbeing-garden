We're in this together!

We realise the important part social interactions play in promoting positive mental health. So we offer the opportunity for you to use the garden as a space to come and connect with others throughout the week. There’s something for everyone at Tedd-Space…from sunrise Tai Chi to the simple pleasure of a good old cup of tea and a chat, we’re here for you. Be sure to follow us on our social channels to keep up to date on the latest, news and upcoming events!

Throughout the year we offer a range of wellbeing classes to focus the mind and nourish the soul. Designed for all abilities, come and experience the benefits of exercising in nature. Check out our timetable to see what’s on offer.

All ages are welcome at Tedd-Space and we believe in encouraging children to explore and engage with nature. Our ‘Growing Together’ classes are designed to give children an early introduction to gardening and wildlife. This is also a great social opportunity for kids and parents alike.
