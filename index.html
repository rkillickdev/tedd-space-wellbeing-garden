Take a breath...

Welcome to Tedd-Space, your wellbeing garden at the heart of our community in Teddington. Situated in the beautiful setting of Royal Bushy Park and open 7 days a week, this is a space for everyone to find a moment of calm in a busy world. We hope the garden brings you what you need, whether that be a quiet place to reflect, the chance to meet your local community, or an opportunity to engage with nature and get your hands dirty!

Minds Matter

Mindfulness is the practice of focusing on the present, free from distraction or judgement and aware of our thoughts and feelings without getting caught up in them. Studies show that developing this skill can help quieten our overtaxed minds and improve mental health. Our wellbeing garden is designed with calm mindful spaces where you can tune into nature, engage your senses and learn to exist in the moment. So come and take the opportunity to reboot your brain.

Community

We hope that Tedd-Space will not just be a garden where we grow plants, but a place where we can grow a community. Over the past few years, the pandemic has shown us how isolation can affect our emotional wellbeing and also the incredible power of people pulling together. So now that we are free to mix and socialise once more, come and take the opportunity to explore our range of activities and connect with those around you.

Belong

Increasingly, health professionals are recognising that gardening can be the perfect vehicle for people to connect. Research shows that those who belong to a community garden feel less isolated and enjoy better physical and mental wellbeing. It provides an area of common ground for people of all ages and backgrounds where life-enhancing connections and friendships can be built. Volunteering at Tedd-Space offers you the chance to become part of our team and help us grow!
