Take a breath...

Welcome to Tedd-Space, your wellbeing garden at the heart of our community in Teddington. Situated in the historic grounds of Strawberry Hill House and open 7 days a week, this is a space for everyone to find a moment of calm in a busy world. We hope the garden brings you what you need, whether that be a quiet place to reflect, the chance to meet your local community, or an opportunity to engage with nature and get your hands dirty!
