We're in this together!

We realise the important part social interactions play in promoting positive mental health. So we offer the opportunity for you to use the garden as a space to come and connect with others throughout the week. There’s something for everyone at Tedd-Space…from sunrise Tai Chi to the simple pleasure of a good old cup of tea and a chat, we’re here for you. Be sure to follow us on our social channels to keep up to date on the latest, news and upcoming events!
